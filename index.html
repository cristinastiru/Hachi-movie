About Movie

The film is a DRAMA inspired by a true story set in Japan in the early part of the 20th century. Richard Gere's character, Professor Parker Wilson, finds a puppy one day when he gets off the train and decides to take it home and keep it, forming an inseparable couple. The dog comes to the station every day at the same time to wait for his owner, and everyone gets to know him.The main characters are the teacher and the dog found at the station. At first, knowing that his wife will not agree to keep him, he puts up notices around town. But everything takes a different turn when she herself tells some of the people who want the dog that the animal she found already has a family, her own, even though Hachi has destroyed a model he worked on for months.
